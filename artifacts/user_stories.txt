1. As a customer, I want to search for flights by destination, departure date, and number of passengers so that I can find the best options for my travel needs.
2. As a customer, I want to compare flight prices from different airlines so that I can choose the most affordable option.
3. As a customer, I want to select and book a flight, including specifying seat preferences and travel insurance so that I can secure my reservation.
4. As a customer, I want to receive confirmation and itinerary details via email so that I have a record of my booking.
5. As an admin, I want to manage flight schedules and inventory so that I can ensure accurate pricing and availability.
6. As an admin, I want to generate reports on booking trends and customer demographics so that I can make informed business decisions.
